.. important::

   On **Ubuntu 22.04** and **Red Hat 8**, every time you enable or disable
   the disclaimer or modify its content, you must run the following command
   as the ``zextras`` user:

      zextras$ /opt/zextras/libexec/zmaltermimeconfig

   On systemd-based OS versions such as **Ubuntu 24.04** and **Red Hat 9**,
   no additional manual action is required.
